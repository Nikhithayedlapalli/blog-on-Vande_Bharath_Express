Vande Bharat Express – The Pride of Modern India

India has taken a major step towards modern and high-speed travel with the Vande Bharat Express, also known as Train 18. Designed and built in India, it represents the spirit of “Make in India” and Atmanirbhar Bharat(Self-Reliant India)..

  • Overview

    Feature Description
    Developer Integral Coach Factory (ICF), Chennai
    Launched Year 2019
    Maximum Speed 180km/h
    Train Type Semi High-Speed (Electric Multiple Unit)
    Power Source 25 kV AC Electric Overhead
    Coach Type Chair Car & Executive Chair Car
    Special Feature Fully Indian-made train under “Make in India”
    Technology Regenerative Braking, Automatic Doors, GPS Info System

    Comfort and Modern Features

    The Vande Bharat Express is designed to give passengers a world-class travel experience with luxury and safety.
      Key features include:
    • Automatic doors with sliding steps
    • Reclining and rotating seats (in Executive class)
    • Wi-Fi and GPS information display
    • Bio-vacuum toilets
    • LED lighting and wide glass windows
    • Regenerative braking for energy savings

    Food Services on Vande Bharat Express

      IRCTC provides two ticket options:
    • With Catering (Meal Included) – Includes tea, snacks, breakfast/lunch/dinner depending on journey time.
    • Without Catering (No Meal) – Cheaper by ₹150–₹250.

    Sample Menu

    • Morning: Tea, bread cutlet, idli, upma, fruit juice
    • Afternoon: Rice, dal, roti, paneer curry, curd, sweet
    • Evening: Tea, snacks, biscuits
      • All meals are prepared hygienically and served hot.

    • Conclusion

    The Vande Bharat Express represents India’s leap into next-generation rail travel — combining speed, comfort, and modern design.
    With reduced journey times, clean coaches, and onboard catering, it offers passengers an experience similar to air travel at affordable prices.
    This initiative strengthens India’s vision of a self-reliant, modern, and connected nation.
